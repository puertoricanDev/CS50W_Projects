Welcome to the plantain spiders recipe page! Now hands on the ingredients(Two Servings):

Ingredients Quantity
Green Plantains 4
Vegetable oil to deepfry
Chicken broth 1 cup
ketchup 1 part
mayo 1 part

  1. Step

    Peel the plantain and cut it into 1-inch chunks.

  2. Step

    Heat the oil in a large skillet. Place the plantains in the oil and fry on both sides, approximately 3 1/2 minutes per side.

  3. Step

    Remove the plantains from the pan and flatten the plantains by placing a plate over the fried plantains and pressing down.

  4. Step

    Dip the plantains in broth or salty water, then return them to the hot oil and fry 1 minute on each side. Salt to taste and serve immediately.

  5. Mayo-ketchup Sauce

    Mix equal parts of mayo and ketchup into a container, add a pinch of salt and for extra flavour a pinch of garlic powder.
